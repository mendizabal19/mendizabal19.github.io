Tambrella

Tambrella is a product designed for an outdoor space, specifically to the banks of a river or a park. This new element it is composed of three elements. Stick steel and two round circumferences. The upper circumference can be raised thanks to a mechanism of screw and nut and users can take advantage of this shadow to use the lower table.

The material used is is polypropylene for the lower table and polypropylene with fiberglass. The stick is made of steel
